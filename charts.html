Opioid Map

Explore country-level consumption trends by opioid class and year

Explore country-level consumption trends by hovering over different geographic location in the Opioid Map. Use the dropdown menu to select specific opioid classes and the Year slider to visualize global consumption patterns for a given year. All opioid consumption levels are reported in milligrams per person.





Regional Trends

Visualize longitudional trends in opioid cosumption by country or region.

Use the selection box at the bottom of the chart to visualize trends by country and opiate class. To download the data shown in the chart, move your mouse over a data point to highlight the series. After you've made your selection, and option to download your data will appear. All country names are abbreviated using standard three letter country codes.



Cognostics

"Cognostics" are computer guiding diagnostics that prioritize the review of exploratory plots generated from large and complex datasets. We provide three cognostic plots that can be used to systematically explore opoiod consumption trends.

Key Trends by Continent

To visualize key consumption trends, provide continent and opiate class information in the selection box below. Then, use the dropdown to choose from alternative cognostics to display in the chart on the right.


Each country in the continent you selected appears as a dot above. Click on a dot to view detailed trends for the country in the chart on the right.

The country you selected on the right chart will appear in the foreground. All other countries appear muted in the background.


Opioid Consumption Clusters

Based on thier opiate conumption data, the chart below uses a statistical method, called multi-dimensional scaling (MDS), to visualize the level of similarity between countries in the same continent, or among multiple continents. To vizualize opiate consumption "clusters" in the chart on the left, indicate continent and opioid classes information in the selection box below.

Hover over a country to display more detailed country and continent-level trends in chart on the right side.

The country you selected on the right chart will appear in the foreground. All other countries appear muted in the background.
